I am a software developer at GreenSync where I work with deX, a distributed energy exchange. deX gives energy retailers and networks the power to "crowd source" energy from people who have solar panels, batteries and other energy resources connected to their homes and businesses. By drawing on these mini power plants, energy retailers and networks can reduce our reliance on gas or coal-powered generators. Our ultimate mission is to empower consumers to buy and sell energy amongst themselves – reducing or eliminating the need for unsustainable energy sources.
